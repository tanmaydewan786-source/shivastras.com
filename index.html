How it could work

A simple left to right flow: wear the ring, tap, breathe, repeat. No apps. No notifications.

  1. wear the ring on a finger that feels natural.
  2. tap it twice when you notice stress or overthinking.
  3. inhale on the first soft cue, exhale on the second.
  4. repeat for three breaths until your body feels a little quieter.
