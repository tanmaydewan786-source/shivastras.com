What Shivastras is

Shivastras is a modern ritual ring concept. Instead of adding more apps and alerts, it imagines a small object you touch when you want to slow down and come back to yourself.

How it could work

A simple four step flow that turns the ring into a tiny calm ritual you can access anywhere.

1
Wear the ring
Choose the finger that feels natural so the ring becomes part of your daily hand movements.
2
Tap twice to start
Two taps become your private signal that you want to shift out of autopilot and into awareness.
3
Let it guide three breaths (concept)
In a future version, the gemstone could respond with soft cues that match three slow breaths.
4
Repeat until it becomes a habit
Over time, the ring itself becomes a reminder that calm is something you can choose on purpose.

This is a vision, not yet a device. The survey tells us which parts to prototype and who we are building for.
